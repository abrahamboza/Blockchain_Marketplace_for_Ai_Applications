About This Platform

This decentralized marketplace allows users to securely share and trade data and machine learning models. All transactions are recorded on a blockchain for transparency and security. Data is encrypted and only accessible to authorized users.

With our integrated machine learning capabilities, you can not only share data but also train models directly on the platform. The entire machine learning process is recorded on the blockchain, creating a verifiable lineage from data to model.
